Match The Colours – a simple reaction time test

Coded this reaction time game using Python. You click on a flashing colour to freeze it, then try to freeze the other colour when they match. The goal is to match the colours in as little time as possible. Warning: flashing colours may not be suitable for photosensitive viewers.

Infestation? Infeastation! (2014) – an eat or be eaten Python game

Coded this game project using Python, included sounds. You play as a small cockroach and you have to eat other cockroaches your size or smaller in order to grow bigger, and you have to avoid the cockroaches larger than you which will eat you instead. The goal is to survive and grow to be the largest cockroach in existence in order to win over all the girls.
